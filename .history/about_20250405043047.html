About INFINIDEX

INFINIDEX was created by voidrane splicer as a non-verbal search engine for the indieweb.

Tired of traditional search boxes? INFINIDEX offers a dynamic feed of user-submitted imagery, each image linking back to the submitter's site. It's a visual exploration of the decentralized web, indexed by the community itself.

This site is designed to help indieweb creators gain visibility and discover new and interesting corners of the internet. By embedding the INFINIDEX widget, users contribute to the network and get their own site indexed.

Hover over images to reveal tags and explore the diverse aesthetics of the indieweb.
